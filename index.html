How it Works

Elevate collects non-invasive health statistics from users like you. We then compare that data against other "earthlings" to give you a baseline fitness level compared to your peers, and show you how fit you are compared to astronauts.

Behind the scenes, NASA uses your anonymized biometric data to look for potential health problems in astronauts, and to build a database that helps the help metrics that may indicate good candidates for space travel in the future
