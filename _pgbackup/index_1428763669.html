Welcome to Elevate

Elevate is a new way to help NASA find the first generation of space-ready colonists while giving you a chance to test your fitness against field-tested astronauts.

Get Started »

How it Works

Elevate collects non-invasive personal data from users like you. We then compare that data against other "earthlings" to give you a baseline fitness level compared to your peers, and show you how fit you are compared to astronauts.

Behind the scenes, NASA uses your anomytized biometric data to look for potential health problems in astronauts, and to build a database that helps the help metrics that may indicate good candidates for space travel in the future
